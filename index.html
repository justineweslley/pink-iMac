Sleek Design and Vibrant Colors

The redesigned iMac comes in a lively palette of green, yellow, orange, pink, purple, blue, and silver. Its ultra-thin build combines style and performance, instantly brightening up any workspace. Each model includes color-coordinated accessories like the Magic Keyboard and Magic Mouse (or optional Magic Trackpad), all now updated with USB-C for easier charging.

Powered by the M4 Chip

At the core of the new iMac is Apple's most advanced silicon yet—the M4 chip. It delivers up to 1.7× faster everyday performance than the M1 model and 2.1× higher speeds for demanding tasks like gaming, video editing, and 3D rendering. With the world's fastest CPU core, a powerful new GPU, and an advanced 16-core Neural Engine, the iMac M4 handles everything from productivity to creative workflows with ease. Up to 32GB of unified memory, it ensures smooth multitasking, responsive apps, and AI-driven features that make every task faster and smarter.

Apple Intelligence: A Smarter Mac Experience

Apple Intelligence brings a smarter, more intuitive Mac experience. It includes systemwide Writing Tools that help you draft, edit, and format content effortlessly, a more capable Siri that understands both text and voice commands, and seamless ChatGPT integration for instant AI-powered assistance on your desktop. Combined with Apple's Private Cloud Compute, all your personal data stays secure and private while you benefit from faster, smarter workflows, smarter app suggestions, and AI-driven automation for creative and productivity tasks.

Stunning 4.5K Retina Display

The 24-inch 4.5K Retina display continues to shine as one of iMac’s best features. A new nano-texture glass option minimizes glare while preserving color accuracy, perfect for any lighting condition. Combined with a six-speaker sound system and three-microphone array, the iMac offers an immersive multimedia experience for work, gaming, and entertainment.

Smarter Video Calls

Featuring a 12MP Center Stage camera, the iMac keeps you perfectly in frame during calls. Its new Desk View mode offers a top-down look at your workspace, making it effortless to showcase projects, share ideas, and create engaging presentations. It is perfect for teachers, creators, and professionals. Perfect for any workspace, designed for effortless productivity.

Advanced Connectivity

Every iMac now includes four USB-C ports with Thunderbolt 4, enabling ultra-fast data transfers and support for up to two 6K external displays. It also features Wi-Fi 6E for lightning-fast wireless internet and Bluetooth 5.3 for seamless device pairing. Touch ID for secure and effortless logins, authorize purchases, and access sensitive files with just a fingerprint, making everyday tasks more convenient.

macOS Sequoia: Designed for the M4

Running on macOS Sequoia, the iMac brings new tools like iPhone Mirroring, improved Safari Highlights, Game Mode, and Personalized Spatial Audio. The new Passwords app keeps credentials safe, while customizable video call backgrounds add a personal touch.

The Next Leap for iMac and the M4 Family

Alongside the 24-inch iMac, Apple revealed the M4 Pro and M4 Max chips, the most powerful in the M4 family. The iMac itself comes with 16GB or 24GB memory and 256GB, 512GB, or 1TB storage, starting at ₱84,990. With its sleek design, 4.5K Retina display, and intelligent features, it blends style, performance, and creativity.

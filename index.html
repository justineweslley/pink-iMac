What makes it special

Discover the innovative features that make the Pink iMac perfect for work, creativity, and everything in between.

Sleek Design and Vibrant Colors

The redesigned iMac comes in a lively palette of green, yellow, orange, pink, purple, blue, and silver. Its ultra-thin build combines style and performance, instantly brightening up any workspace. Each model includes color-coordinated accessories like the Magic Keyboard and Magic Mouse (or optional Magic Trackpad), all now updated with USB-C for easier charging.

Powered by the M4 Chip

At the core of the new iMac is Apple's most advanced silicon yet—the M4 chip. It delivers up to 1.7× faster everyday performance than the M1 model and 2.1× higher speeds for demanding tasks like gaming, video editing, and 3D rendering. With the world's fastest CPU core, a powerful new GPU, and an advanced 16-core Neural Engine, the iMac M4 handles everything from productivity to creative workflows with ease.

Apple Intelligence: A Smarter Mac Experience

Apple Intelligence brings a smarter, more intuitive Mac experience. It includes systemwide Writing Tools that help you draft, edit, and format content effortlessly, a more capable Siri that understands both text and voice commands, and seamless ChatGPT integration for instant AI-powered assistance on your desktop.

Stunning 4.5K Retina Display

The 24-inch 4.5K Retina display continues to shine as one of iMac's best features. A new nano-texture glass option minimizes glare while preserving color accuracy, perfect for any lighting condition.

Smarter Video Calls

Featuring a 12MP Center Stage camera, the iMac keeps you perfectly in frame during calls. Its new Desk View mode offers a top-down look at your workspace, making it effortless to showcase projects and create engaging presentations.

Advanced Connectivity

Every iMac now includes four USB-C ports with Thunderbolt 4, enabling ultra-fast data transfers and support for up to two 6K external displays. It also features Wi-Fi 6E for lightning-fast wireless internet and Bluetooth 5.3 for seamless device pairing.

macOS Sequoia: Designed for the M4

Running on macOS Sequoia, the iMac brings new tools like iPhone Mirroring, improved Safari Highlights, Game Mode, and Personalized Spatial Audio. The new Passwords app keeps credentials safe, while customizable video call backgrounds add a personal touch.

The Next Leap for iMac and the M4 Family

Alongside the 24-inch iMac, Apple revealed the M4 Pro and M4 Max chips, the most powerful in the M4 family. The iMac itself comes with 16GB or 24GB memory and 256GB, 512GB, or 1TB storage, starting at ₱84,990. With its sleek design, 4.5K Retina display, and intelligent features, it blends style, performance, and creativity.

Built for the Planet

Aligned with Apple's 2030 carbon-neutral goal, the new iMac is crafted from 100% recycled aluminum and meets strict energy efficiency standards. Apple is already carbon neutral, and by 2030, every product you love will be too, with reduced emissions from manufacturing, transportation, and energy use. This brings cutting-edge technology and sustainability together for a better future.
